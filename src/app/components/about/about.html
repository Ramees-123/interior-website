Andspacio is a premium interior design studio based in Calicut, Kerala. We specialize in creating sophisticated spaces that reflect the unique personalities and lifestyles of our clients.

With over 10 years of experience, our team of talented designers combines creativity, functionality, and attention to detail to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments. From residential homes to commercial spaces, we bring the same level of passion and commitment to every project.

We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone. Our approach is collaborative, ensuring that your vision becomes a reality while we guide you through every step of the design process.
